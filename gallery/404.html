The cat, rendered
many ways.

A gallery of small, real-time experiments. Each is one creature seen through a different lens — wind-blown fur, halftone ink — and each is alive to the pointer. Open one and handle it.

Each piece is a self-contained Canvas / WebGL sketch behind one contract; the shell only lists and routes. Static-first, written from scratch — no UI framework for the art.

No cookies, no tracking — only an anonymous note of whether your browser ran the live piece or its still fallback.
